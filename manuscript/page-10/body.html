Champions Trophy 2017

Champion's Trophy would be held in England from 1st June 2017 to 18th June 2017. During this time Virat will be undergoing the period of Rahu mahadasha and Saturn antardasha.

Rahu from 3rd House is aspecting 9th House of fortune, luck, travels etc. Saturn is 3rd Lord of courage, efforts and 2nd Lord of prosperity, it is aspecting 7th House of gain of position, foreign relations and 10th House of career and profession.

During Saturn's transit, retrograde Jupiter will be in 10th House and from here shall be aspecting the natal Jupiter in 6th House of competition. This indicates a fairly successful Champions Trophy for Virat Kohli.
